Adding an Apple iOS Device

Step 1: Device Prerequisites
In order to add your iOS device, you must be running iOS 11.1 or higher. Check the operating system of your device and upgrade if necessary.
Step 2: Install AirWatch Agent
Prior to adding your device, it must have AirWatch Agent installed from the App Store.
Step 3: Choose your Authentication Route
To add your device, you must authenticate using either: your corporate email address or a QR token provided to your corporate email.
Authenticating with corporate email:
Open the AirWatch Agent application on your device and select the option for "Email Address". When prompted, provide your corporate email address into that field (example: joesmith@its.jnj.com). If successful, you will be redirected to PING where you will need to provide your network username and password to finish authentication. Once authenticated, AirWatch will finish setting your device up.
Authenticating with QR token:
Navigate to either [url to my-device] or [url to IRIS] and submit a request to add a new device to AirWatch. Complete the request and you will automatically recieve an email in your inbox within a few minutes with a QR code. Once you have the email, open AirWatch and select QR token.

What Apple iOS devices are supported by J&J?

Supported Devices
J&J requires that an iOS device run at a minimum iOS 11.1 in order to be managed. The following devices support iOS 11.1 and higher:
  • iPhone: iPhone X, iPhone 8, iPhone 8 Plus, iPhone 7, iPhone 7 Plus, iPhone 6s, iPhone 6s Plus, iPhone 6, iPhone 6 Plus, iPhone SE, iPhone 5s
  • iPad: 12.9-inch iPad Pro 2nd generation, 12.9-inch iPad Pro 1st generation, 10.5-inch iPad Pro, 9.7-inch iPad Pro, iPad Air 2, iPad Air, iPad 5th generation, iPad mini 4, iPad mini 3, iPad mini 2
  • iPod: iPod touch 6th generation

Why should I add my device?

Why add my device?
Having your device managed by J&J ensures that you may securely access J&J email, apps, and data on your device. Your device will receive access to email, the J&J app store, and profiles to allow your device to automatically connect to JJ Wi-Fi. It is highly encouraged that anyone accessing J&J data on their device have their device managed by J&J to ensure the device is properly secured.
